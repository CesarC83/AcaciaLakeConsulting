About the Firm

Discretion. Precision. Results.

Acacia Lake Consulting is a veteran-owned firm founded on a single belief: that the most consequential decisions in business deserve advisors who treat them with the gravity they deserve. We bring decades of cross-disciplinary expertise to every engagement — pairing strategic clarity with operational discipline.

Whether guiding an early-stage founder through their first capital raise, hardening a law firm's digital infrastructure, or coordinating emergency response on an active oilfield site, we operate as a trusted extension of your team. Our work is quiet, our outcomes loud.

We don't sell deliverables. We deliver judgment.
